To use as a coach/third party:

1. Simply visit http://exsports-analytics.herokuapp.com/

To explore our repository:

1. Fork this Repository.
2. Clone your Fork on a different branch:
    git clone -b <name-of-branch> https://github.com/nachiketbhuta/exsports-analytics.git
3. After adding any feature:
    Goto your fork and create a pull request.
4. We will test your modifications and merge changes.
